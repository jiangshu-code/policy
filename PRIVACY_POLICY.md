## 中文版

### 1. 概述

「护眼计时」（以下简称"本应用"）是一款帮助用户遵循 20-20-20 用眼法则的本地计时与提醒工具。我们高度重视您的隐私。本应用在设计上即不收集、不存储、不上传任何可识别您个人身份的信息。

### 2. 我们不收集的信息

本应用**不会**收集、记录或上传以下任何信息：

- 您的姓名、电子邮件、电话号码或任何账户信息（本应用不需要注册或登录）
- 您的位置信息
- 您的通讯录、照片、相机、麦克风或健康数据
- 您的设备标识符（IDFA、IDFV 等）用于广告追踪
- 您的使用行为、崩溃日志或任何形式的分析数据
- 任何 Cookie 或类似追踪技术

### 3. 本地存储

为了提供基本功能，本应用会在您的设备上本地保存以下信息，这些信息**不会**离开您的设备，也不会被传输到任何服务器：

- **计时时长偏好设置**：保存在本地以便下次启动时恢复您的设置。

您可以通过卸载本应用随时删除上述本地数据。

### 4. 系统权限

本应用仅在您主动启用时请求以下系统权限：

- **通知权限（Notifications）**：用于在您设定的时间间隔后向您本机发送本地提醒，提示您休息眼睛。所有通知均为本地通知（Local Notification），不涉及任何远程推送服务，不会向任何服务器发送数据。

您可以随时在 iOS「设置 > 通知 > 护眼计时」中关闭通知权限，这不会影响本应用计时功能的使用。

### 5. 第三方服务

本应用**不集成**任何第三方 SDK、分析工具、广告网络或社交登录服务。

### 6. 儿童隐私

本应用不收集任何信息，因此适合所有年龄段的用户使用，包括 13 岁以下儿童。

### 7. 数据安全

由于本应用不收集或传输任何个人数据，您的信息天然处于您设备的本地安全环境中，受 iOS 系统沙盒机制保护。

### 8. 隐私政策的变更

如本应用未来功能发生变化导致本政策需要更新，我们将通过应用更新和 App Store 的版本说明告知您。建议您定期查阅本政策。

### 9. 联系我们

如您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过 App Store 的开发者联系方式与我们联系。

## English Version

### 1. Overview

"protecteye" (the "App") is a local timer and reminder utility that helps users follow the 20-20-20 eye-care rule. We take your privacy seriously. By design, the App does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

### 2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does **not** collect, log, or upload any of the following:

- Your name, email, phone number, or any account information (no sign-up or login is required)
- Your location
- Your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or health data
- Your device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, etc.) for advertising tracking
- Your usage behavior, crash logs, or any analytics data
- Any cookies or similar tracking technologies

### 3. Local Storage

To provide its basic functionality, the App stores the following on your device locally. This data **never** leaves your device and is **never** transmitted to any server:

- **Timer duration preference**, saved locally so your setting is restored on next launch.

You can delete this local data at any time by uninstalling the App.

### 4. System Permissions

The App requests the following system permission only when you actively enable it:

- **Notifications**: Used to deliver on-device local reminders to rest your eyes after your chosen interval. All notifications are local; no remote push services are used, and no data is sent to any server.

You may disable notifications at any time in **iOS Settings > Notifications > 护眼计时**. The App's timer functionality will continue to work.

### 5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates **no** third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or social login services.

### 6. Children's Privacy

The App collects no information and is therefore suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13.

### 7. Data Security

Because the App does not collect or transmit any personal data, your information remains within your device's local sandbox, protected by iOS.

### 8. Changes to This Policy

If future functionality requires updates to this policy, we will notify you via app updates and App Store release notes. Please review this policy periodically.

### 9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the contact information listed on the App Store.
